Making private lessons
work for Ireland

LevelUp! was built because booking a grind, a coaching session, or a music lesson shouldn't require WhatsApp negotiations. We built the platform that makes availability visible and booking instant.

The problem we solve

Private lessons in Ireland are still arranged through informal channels — WhatsApp, text, email. Repeated back-and-forth messaging leads to missed bookings, scheduling conflicts, and income uncertainty for instructors. LevelUp! replaces all of that with a structured, transparent booking system.

Three categories, one platform

We support academic grinds (Maths, Irish, English and more), sports coaching (football, padel, personal training), and music lessons (guitar, piano, violin) — all with the same clean booking workflow.

Booking for under 18s?

LevelUp! allows parents and guardians to manage bookings for children under 18, ensuring a safe and secure experience for younger users. You can see Teachers and Coaches who are verified to work with under 18s. If you book with an unverified Teacher or Coach it should be know that is on the Parent/Guardians descretion.

Built by DCU students

LevelUp! is a project built by Dylan Doyle and James Dalton as part of the CSC1180 Computing for Business module at Dublin City University. It addresses a genuine market gap identified through primary research with students, parents, and teachers across Ireland.
